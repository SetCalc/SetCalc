# SetCalc

**Free settlement calculator and open personal injury settlement data.**

[SetCalc](https://setcalc.com) helps injury victims understand what their claim may be worth. The settlement calculator combines the details of a claim (injury type, medical treatment, fault, insurance coverage, and state) with data from thousands of real verdicts and settlements to produce a realistic settlement range, free.

## Open data

We publish our verdicts and settlements database as open data:

- [personal-injury-verdicts-settlements-data](https://github.com/SetCalc/personal-injury-verdicts-settlements-data): 3,099 US personal injury verdicts and settlements with state, year, case type, injury, and amount for every record. CC BY 4.0, refreshed from the live database. Includes CSV, JSON, a data dictionary, and a free read-only API.
